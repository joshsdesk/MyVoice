**A translator for your child's unique voice.**

![App Banner --- placeholder](./docs/images/banner_placeholder.png)

MyVoice is an open-source tool that helps parents and caregivers
**understand and translate their child's unique speech patterns**.\
Many nonverbal or minimally verbal children --- especially in the autism
community --- communicate using **consistent personal sounds** that
don't match standard English but **have real meaning**.

MyVoice lets you:

-   Record what your child actually says\
-   Label what it means\
-   Build a personal dictionary\
-   Translate those sounds for others

It works alongside AAC devices, acting as a bridge between your child's
voice and the outside world.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## üéØ The Problem

Children often speak in ways that don't align with typical language, but
their speech is:

-   Consistent\
-   Intentional\
-   Communicative

The challenge: others may not understand these personalized sounds.

MyVoice creates a space to **capture**, **store**, and **translate**
your child's real voice so caregivers, teachers, and family can
understand them.

## ‚ú® Current Features (Building Phase)

-   **Child Profile**\
    Create and manage a secure local profile.

-   **Personal Dictionary**\
    Record your child's unique sounds, words, or phrases.

-   **Meaning Mapping**\
    Label each recording with the English word or phrase it represents.

-   **Playback**\
    Hear the child's original sound --- perfect for sharing with others.

-   **Local-First Storage**\
    All data stays on your device.

## üöÄ Getting Started

### Prerequisites

-   Flutter SDK (3.10.1+ recommended)\
-   Android tablet or iOS device\
-   Git

### Installation

1.  Clone the repo
    `bash     git clone https://github.com/joshsdesk/MyVoice.git     cd MyVoice`

2.  Install dependencies `bash     flutter pub get`

3.  Run on your device `bash     flutter run`

If your device doesn't appear, run:

``` bash
flutter doctor -v
```
